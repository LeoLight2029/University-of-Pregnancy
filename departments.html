The Placenta Department studies nutrient transfer, fetal support systems, and hormonal regulation throughout pregnancy.

Testicles Department

Researches sperm formation, genetic contribution, and the hormonal systems involved in conception.

Studies ovulation, follicle development, egg physiology, and reproductive hormone cycles.

Explores uterine structure, implantation, fetal housing, and labor-related anatomy.
